    Qualifying for our best chargers list is this 48 amp, hardwired level 2 EV charger by Pass & Seymour. One of the brilliant features about this charger is its flexibility in how much or how little energy you choose to use. Depending on your vehicle’s needs and your energy consumption preferences, it gives you the ability to set the usage limit to as low as 16 amps and as high as 48 amps.

    Featuring an 18-foot charging cable, this sleek and compact unit uses LED lights to indicate charging status, so you can easily know if your EV is charging, fully charged or if there is a charging issue, all at a glance. Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, the unit comes with a storage hook, holster, and a flexible conduit kit and because there’s no programming, commissioning or complicated set up required, it’s very easy to install it in your garage, carport or driveway.
